# ⚔️ **StrengthSMP | Professional Strength PvP System**

**StrengthSMP** is not your typical PvP plugin. It is a complete competitive combat overhaul for Spigot/Paper 1.21+ servers.

Designed for hardcore SMPs, Lifesteal, and Factions servers, this plugin introduces dynamic strength-stealing mechanics, RPG-style weapon abilities, and a Monarch leaderboard system to create intense, server-wide PvP.

**Win fights to gain strength. Lose fights and watch it slip away.**

## ✨ Core Features

### 🩸 **Dynamic Strength Stealing & Scaling**
Players start with a default strength level. Killing players lets you steal their strength.

* **Damage Scaling:** Strength directly affects damage output and damage reduction.
* **Underdog System:** Weaker players gain bonus multipliers when defeating stronger opponents.
* **Physical Withdrawals:** Convert strength into items (Nautilus Shells) to trade or store safely.

Every weapon has its own identity and playstyle with passive bonuses and powerful active abilities (**Right-Click**):

## 👑 Monarch System & Bounties

* **Monarch System:** The top killer becomes the Monarch and is targeted server-wide.
* **Bounties:** Place item-based bounties using `/bounty` to create PvP hunts.

![Bounty System](https://i.postimg.cc/B6h1D8yg/bounty-ezgif-com-optimize.gif)

---

## 🛡️ Balanced & Controlled Combat

Built-in systems ensure fair gameplay and prevent abuse:

* **Totem Limits:** Restricts Totems of Undying usage.
* **Anti-Farming:** Prevents repeated kills of the same player for farming.
* **Potion Ban:** Disables vanilla Strength potions.

![Totem Limit](https://i.postimg.cc/zBcWRbHX/totemgif-ezgif-com-optimize.gif)
